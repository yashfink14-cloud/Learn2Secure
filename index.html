Top 5 Safety Tips

  • Use strong, unique passwords + a manager.
  • Turn on two-factor authentication.
  • Beware of links & attachments.
  • Keep devices and apps updated.
  • Backup important data regularly.

Emergency?

For Indian users, report incidents at cybercrime.gov.in or dial 1930 (Financial Cyber Fraud helpline).
